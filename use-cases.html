Use cases

SyMptOMS is a set of software tools that facilitates the creation of mobile applications with geospatial functionalities tailored to each patient and treatment. The platform has already been used in three use cases. All patients have shown positive progress throughout their treatment

Panic Disorder & Agoraphobia

47-year-old man in panic and agoraphobia since childhood. He have received discontinued prior treatment without improvement. He has been 4 months under treatment.

Problem gambling

28-year-old man with problem gambling. He has not received previous treatment. He did not have a great economic loss but has a slight interference with his partner and feeling of uncontrol. The goal is to stop playing. He has been 2,5 months under treatment.

Pathological gambling

46-year-old man with pathological gambling who has not recieved previous treatment. He has great economic losses and interference in his family life. The goal is to stop playing. He has been 1.5 months under treatment.
